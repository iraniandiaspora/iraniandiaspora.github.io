The United States has the largest Iranian immigrant population. However, sizable Iranian communities exist across Europe, Canada, Australia, Türkiye, and Israel. An estimated 1.76 million Iran-born individuals reside outside Iran as of 2024, based on UN International Migrant Stock data supplemented by Eurostat where UN coverage is incomplete.
These estimates count people born in Iran who reside in another country. Second-generation Iranians born in the destination country are not included. Figures are derived from each country’s census or population register data on foreign-born residents.
