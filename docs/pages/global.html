The United States has the largest Iranian immigrant population. However, sizable Iranian communities exist across Europe, Canada, Australia, Türkiye, and Israel. According to the UN, 1.73 million Iran-born individuals reside outside Iran as of 2024.
These estimates count people born in Iran who reside in another country. Second-generation Iranians born in the destination country are not included. The UN derives these figures from each country’s census or population register data on foreign-born residents.
