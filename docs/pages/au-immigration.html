Iranian migration to Australia was minimal before 1980, with only 2,117 Iran-born residents arriving before that year. The 1980s saw a sharp increase to 6,831 arrivals, and numbers continued rising through each subsequent period.
The peak period was 2011–2015, when 24,624 Iran-born individuals arrived—more than a third of the current Iran-born population. Annual arrivals ranged between 2,800 and 3,500 from 2016 to 2019, then fell to 1,283 in 2020 and 727 in 2021.

Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics — Census of Population and Housing, 2021
Iran-born population enumerated via country of birth question.
