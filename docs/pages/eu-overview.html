Estimated Iran-Born Population in Europe

Summed across 12 European countries with published data

A person is counted if they were born in Iran and currently live in one of:

  • Eurostat — 8 European countries (country-of-birth tables)
  • Destatis Mikrozensus 2024 — Germany
  • ONS Census 2021, NRS Scotland's Census 2022, NISRA NI Census 2021 — United Kingdom (combined)
  • Swiss Federal Statistical Office (BFS) — Switzerland (STATPOP register)
