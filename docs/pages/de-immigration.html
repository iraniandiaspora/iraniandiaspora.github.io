About 167,000 Iranian-origin residents hold German citizenship (roughly 52% of the total), and about 152,000 are still Iranian nationals. BAMF recorded 7,840 Iranian naturalizations in 2024.
Annual Iranian arrivals to Germany show two distinct waves: a steady background flow in the 1990s and 2000s of around 3,000–8,000 per year, and a sharp increase from 2015 onwards peaking above 21,000 in 2016 and again in 2023. The Mikrozensus records that about 44% of first-generation residents came through the asylum system.

Source: Destatis — Mikrozensus 2024 Erstergebnisse
