Iranian immigration to Canada surged in the 2000s and 2010s. About half of first-generation Iranian-Canadians arrived in 2006 or later, while the 1980s revolution-and-war era accounts for only 8% of the current first-generation population.
About 60% of Iranian-Canadians are naturalized citizens, while 27% have not yet obtained citizenship. Economic immigration has been the primary pathway since the 1990s.
