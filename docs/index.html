The Iranian Diaspora Dashboard is part of the Iranian Diaspora project funded by the Ebrahimi Program on Iranian Diaspora in Global Perspective, housed at the UCLA Center for Near Eastern Studies (CNES). It was created by Roxanne Corbeil, Kevan Harris, and Gilad Wenig. For any inquiries or assistance, please contact cnes@international.ucla.edu.

What’s changed

April 2026

  • Updated all U.S. data to the 2020–2024 American Community Survey (previously 2018–2022)
  • U.S. population estimate revised to 794,915. Now includes children of Iranian parents identified through household data, and a new “Iranian” race write-in category first available in the 2024 survey
  • Income pages for both countries now use pre-tax household income deciles, ranked against each country’s national population (ages 25–54), enabling direct cross-country comparison
  • Redesigned the site for faster loading and improved interactivity

September 2025

  • Added Canada pages using the 2021 Canadian Census
  • New sections on language, religion, education, employment, and income for Iranian-Canadians

April 2024

  • Dashboard launched with United States and Global data
